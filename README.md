## 📌 Introduction

**TOTAL CARE** is a web-based Life Insurance Management System designed to simplify and modernize traditional life insurance operations.

The system provides a centralized platform where customers, insurance agents, administrators, claims officers, support staff, and auditors can manage insurance-related activities through a single web interface.

It helps reduce manual paperwork, improve service efficiency, maintain accurate records, and provide better transparency between customers and insurance providers.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Design and develop a secure web-based life insurance management system.
- Allow customers to register, log in, view policies, submit claims, and track payments.
- Help staff and administrators manage users, policies, claims, payments, tickets, and feedback.
- Implement role-based access control for different system users.
- Reduce manual processes, paperwork, and delays in insurance operations.
- Maintain structured and reliable records using a relational database.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 **User Management** — registration, login, profile updates, and role-based access.
- 📄 **Policy Management** — create, update, delete, assign, search, and view insurance policies.
- 💳 **Premium Payment Handling** — record payments, track payment status, and maintain payment history.
- 🧾 **Claims Management** — submit claims, update claim status, and track approval or rejection.
- 🎫 **Customer Support System** — create support tickets, view replies, update ticket status, and manage complaints.
- ⭐ **Feedback Management** — collect customer feedback and allow staff/admin responses.
- 📊 **System Audit & Reporting** — monitor system activity, users, payments, policies, claims, and feedback.

## 🔄 Life Insurance System Process

```text
Customer Registration / Login
        ↓
Customer Views Available Policies
        ↓
Customer Selects or Is Assigned a Policy
        ↓
Premium Payment Records Are Managed
        ↓
Customer Submits Claim if Needed
        ↓
Agent / Admin Reviews Claim
        ↓
Claim Status Updated as Pending / Approved / Rejected
        ↓
Customer Can Create Support Tickets or Submit Feedback
        ↓
Auditor / Admin Reviews Reports and System Activity
```

## 👥 Roles and Main Functions

| Role | Main Functions |
|---|---|
| Customer | Register, log in, view policies, make payments, submit claims, create support tickets, and submit feedback. |
| Insurance Agent / Staff | Assist customers, manage assigned tasks, verify details, and support insurance operations. |
| Claims Officer | Review submitted claims and update claim status as pending, approved, or rejected. |
| Administrator | Manage users, policies, payments, claims, tickets, feedback, and overall system data. |
| Support Staff | Respond to customer inquiries, manage tickets, and close or escalate issues. |
| Auditor | Monitor system activity, review reports, analyze feedback, and support transparency. |
